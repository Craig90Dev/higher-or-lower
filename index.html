Lucky Roll Dice Game

Fancy your luck?

  1. Click the Start button to roll the Dice
  2. Guess if the next roll will be higher or lower than the previous roll
  3. The next number will never be the same as the current number
  4. Each correct answer gives you a point to your current streak
  5. If you get it wrong you will lose your streak
  6. Click reset to start the game again, but careful, you will lose your best streak score
  7. Try and get the highest streak!
